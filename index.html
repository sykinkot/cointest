Introducing CoinSupply

The first pure crypto aggregator of depth and scope of crypto currencies and tokens. CoinSupply aggregates the crypto-to-crypto listings across the largest exchanges, allowing users to reach any crypto asset on the market in just a few clicks.

The platform also introduces cutting edge crypto analytics, such as blockchain performance monitoring, exposure to cryptographic algorithms, simple wallet tracking and many more.

With our revolutionary sorting algorithms, users will be able to search and select crypto tokens and currencies based on their qualitative characteristics, such as industry, underlying technology, geographic location and more.

Vision

Since the advent of Bitcoin and Blockchain in 2009, cryptocurrencies were labeled as a financial playtool for geeks and tech savvy users. Now, 8 years later we are witnessing the world being drastically reshaped by the crypto economy.

The interest in blockchain has exploded, showing trust in the technology and willingness of people to be involved. However the learning curve is still too steep for most.

Vision

The lack of simplicity and transparency has made room for fraud, misinformation and is jeopardising the mainstream adoption of crypto assets. The time for change is now.

With current exchanges being inefficient and rapidly becoming obsolete, CoinSupply introduces the next generation infrastructure for the mainstream usage of cryptoy assets.

Vision
Simplicity

The purpose of transitioning to a crypto economy is to simplify things and not complicate them. Users should seamlessly trade, manage and monitor their crypto assets, all in one place, in just a few clicks.

Information should come in a structured way, readily available and easy to understand.

A well crafted user experience must grant intuitive access to the crypto markets without wasting time to figure out the basics.

The goal is to make crypto as easy and simple as its supposed to be!

Vision
Transparency

Transparency is the key to modern crypto economy. Users must have full control over their assets instead of relying on third parties operating behind closed doors.

Easy access to verifiable information, details of users actions and transactions, absence of hidden fees and conditions are fundamental to what CoinSupply stands for.

Vision
Security

Digital assets can be stolen in a matter of minutes! Hacks can be executed via the network, the business entity operating on the network or even on an individual user.

By applying multi-factor action confirmation, cold storages, multi-signature transactions, among other features, CoinSupply sets a new industry standard for operational security.

In case of suspicious activity on the network, CoinSupply will ensure immediate alerts and recommended actions.

Our mission is to educate the community on how to keep their assets safe.
